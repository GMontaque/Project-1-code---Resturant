<About/>

I take pride in being good at thinking on my feet. I like problem solving and have always been good in a crisis where cool heads are needed. Both in my work and in my interests, I often find myself in leading roles which require focus, strong time management and the ability to multi-task whilst taking the team with you.

My passion for IT and computers started at an early age, as a number of family members were involved in IT. At school, I found that IT was a subject I not only enjoyed but excelled at, and it was always a topic discussed at home. Though I continued with IT as a hobby, I pursued a career in the military which I had to abandon due to a rare allergy which prevented me from joining. At this point I initiated online courses in web development whilst holding down a full time job and front end web development was a natural progression from that.
